# Arabic Phrases & AI Translator - Privacy Policy

INTRODUCTION:

Arabic Phrases & AI Transaltor respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected when you use the app.

By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.


INFORMATION COLLECTED:

This app may collect the following types of data only to provide core app functionality.


1. User Content:

This includes:
- Text entered into the AI Translator
- Saved phrases created by the user
- Optional images uploaded for translation

Purpose:
Used solely to provide translation and phrasebook features within the app.

Data Handling:
- This content is not used for advertising
- This content is not used for tracking
- This content is not shared with any third parties for marketing


2. User Identifier (Sync Code):

The app allows users to create a sync code to back up and restore saved phrases.

Purpose:
- Cloud backup and restore
- Ensuring saved phrases can be recovered after reinstalling the app or changing devices

Data Handling:
- The sync code is not linked to your real identity
- No name, email address, or personal information is required
- The app does not create user profiles


3. Photos or Images (Optional):

If you choose to upload an image for translation:

Purpose:
- Image-based translation funtionality

Data Handling:
- Images are processed only to provide translation
- Images are not stored permanently
- Images are not used for training, tracking, or analytics


HOW DATA IS USED:

All collected data is used only for app functionality, including:
- Providing translations
- Saving and restoring phrases
- Improving the reliability of the app

The app DOES NOT:
- Track users across apps or websites
- Sell user data
- Display personalised advertising


DATA SHARING:

Your data is NOT sold or shared with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Limited third-party services may process data only to enable app functionality, such as:
- AI translation services
- Secure cloud storage for backups

These services are used strictly to operate the app.


DATA RETENTION:

- Text and image content is retained only as long as necessary to provide app features
- Users can clear saved phrases at any time within the app
- Removing the app deletes locally stored data


CHILDREN'S PRIVACY:

Arabic Phrases & AI Translator DOES NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.


YOUR CHOICES:

You can:
- Use the phrasebook offline
- Choose whether to upload images
- Choose whether to enable cloud backup
- Clear saved data at any time


CHANGES TO THIS POLICY:

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
